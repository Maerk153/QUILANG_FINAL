About AutoGame Hub

Learn more about our mission and what drives us

Our Story

AutoGame Hub was founded by a group of college students who shared a passion for both automotive engineering and video games. We noticed that these two interests often overlap, with many car enthusiasts also being avid gamers, and vice versa.

Our mission is to create a space where these communities can come together, share knowledge, and celebrate their shared interests.

What We Believe

We believe that the worlds of automotive technology and gaming have more in common than people realize. Both fields push the boundaries of innovation, require precision and skill, and provide thrilling experiences.

Whether you're interested in the latest hypercar technology or the newest racing simulator, AutoGame Hub is your go-to resource.
